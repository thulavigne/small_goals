Small Goals is a simple app that lets you set your own small goals and list the steps you need to take in order to reach your goals.

Created With:

- Rails 3.2.13
- Ruby 2.0.0

Get Started:

- Sign up
- Confirm account by clicking on link provided in confirmation email
- Sign in
- Create new goal
- Add steps within your goal. Steps will help you keep track of things you need to do in order to achieve your small goals.

Remember:

Small Goals is meant to help you achieve literally very small goals. It's the small wins that really matter in life.
